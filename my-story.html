Designing the Future

At 26, I'm studying Design Leadership for my master's at Technische Hochschule Ingolstadt. Alongside that, I'm a product designer at #bastoy in Regensburg, building real products with a social impact.

Before Germany, I designed for Torob, one of Iran's biggest e commerce platforms, and earlier I was at Baloot, a fintech startup where speed and clarity really mattered. Across these roles, I've helped shape four live apps used by more than 30 million people each month.

I work best where UX, UI, data, and research meet, turning complex problems into simple flows people enjoy using. I'm still early in my journey, but I'm here to keep learning, shipping, and making design that truly helps.

Bridging Passions

My journey started with a degree in computer engineering. This background has given me a foundation for my work in product design. Teaching System Analysis and Design as a teaching assistant helped me see the connections between technical systems and digital product design. I love how my engineering skills enhance my design work, making me a better problem-solver.

A Musical Heartbeat

From a young age, I've had a deep love for Iranian traditional music. Playing the tar and setar has always been my favorite pastime. The melodies I create fill my heart with joy and calm my mind. Music isn't just a hobby; it's a way to express my soul.

Cooking is my passion. I love exploring the world of tastes and creating new dishes. Experimenting with flavors and ingredients in the kitchen is a creative process that parallels my work in design. Just like in design, I enjoy mixing and matching until I find the perfect combination.

The Thrill of the Game

Sports are another big part of my life. I love playing football and tennis. I'm a huge fan of Real Madrid and Persepolis football teams. Watching their games fills me with excitement. Stories of the Past

When I'm not playing music, you can find me buried in a historical novel or a book of poetry. The stories of the past fascinate me, and I love losing myself in the rich tapestry of history. Every book takes me on a new adventure, sparking my imagination and creativity.
