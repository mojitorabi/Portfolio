My Story

A bit more about me.

From pixels to melodies, I love merging engineering precision with creative flair. My journey moves between systems, culture, design craft, and curiosity.

Designing the Future

At 26, I'm studying Design Leadership for my master's at Technische Hochschule Ingolstadt. Alongside that, I'm a product designer at #bastoy in Regensburg, building real products with social impact.

Before Germany, I designed for Torob, one of Iran's biggest e commerce platforms, and earlier I was at Baloot, a fintech startup where speed and clarity really mattered. Across these roles, I've helped shape live apps used by more than 30 million people each month.

I work best where UX, UI, data, and research meet, turning complex problems into simple flows people enjoy using.

Bridging Passions

My journey started with a degree in computer engineering. Teaching System Analysis and Design as a teaching assistant helped me see stronger connections between technical systems and digital product design.

A Musical Heartbeat

From a young age, I've had a deep love for Iranian traditional music. Playing tar and setar has always been my favorite pastime. Music isn't just a hobby for me, it's a way to express my soul.
