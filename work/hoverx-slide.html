Project Design Creation, 2024 to 2025

HoverX

A motorsport concept for the year 2100: electromagnetic racing, artificial intelligence, and sustainable energy in one vision.

  • Concept. Hover-motorcycle racing on superconducting tracks: strong performance with a smaller footprint than combustion-era motorsport.
  • Technology. AI-assisted race strategy, predictive safety systems, and grid-scale clean power tied to the circuit infrastructure.
  • Experience. A spectator and driver narrative built around speed, clarity, and trust in human–machine collaboration.
